About Me

Hi there! My name is Sara, and if you're reading this page, I'll assume you're thinking about hiring me, justifying why you don't want to hire me, or you're my mother (hi mom).

Paul Graham once said "Instead of working back from a goal, work forward from promising situations", which succinctly describes my approach to life (and travel). As a child I wanted to be a cartoonist, then a forensic pathologist (I don't even know what that one was about). I went to grad school for Urban and Environmental Planning, and realized I liked data more than the planning process. From there I worked as a BI Analyst (title: Geographer), Software Engineer, Customer Success Engineer, and Engineering Product Manager. And now, UX Designer.

I'm excited to see what promising situations arise next, and hope our journeys can overlap!
